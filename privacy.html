Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Clip Pilot handles information when its operator connects social-media accounts and publishes content.

1. Information processed

Clip Pilot processes videos, captions, hashtags, publishing settings, posting history, and error information supplied by the operator. When an account is connected, the relevant platform may provide an OAuth access token, refresh token, account identifier, display name, and platform-specific posting capabilities.

2. How information is used

Information is used only to prepare videos, display the connected account, upload content selected by the operator, check publishing status, and maintain local posting history.

3. Local storage and security

Clip Pilot is designed as a local application. Video files, history, settings, and OAuth tokens are stored on the operator's computer. OAuth token payloads are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with an encryption key controlled by the operator. Clip Pilot does not collect or store social-media passwords.

4. Information shared with platforms

When the operator explicitly connects an account or posts content, Clip Pilot transmits the necessary information to the selected service through its official API. This can include the video, caption, hashtags, privacy selection, thumbnail, and OAuth authorization. Platform handling is governed by the applicable policies of TikTok, Google/YouTube, and Meta/Instagram.

5. Selling and advertising

Clip Pilot does not sell personal information and does not use personal information for targeted advertising.

6. Retention and deletion

Information remains on the operator's computer until the operator removes the files, application database, or account authorization. Platform access can also be revoked from the connected platform's account settings.

7. Children's privacy

Clip Pilot is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended to collect children's personal information.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated when the application or platform requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

9. Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the Clip Pilot contact page.
