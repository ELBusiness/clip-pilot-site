Local-first publishing

Prepare once.
Publish responsibly.

Clip Pilot helps its operator prepare and publish their own short-form videos to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels through official platform APIs.

Official connections

Account access uses OAuth. Clip Pilot does not request or store social-media passwords.

Local processing

Videos are prepared with FFmpeg and stored on the operator's own computer.

User controlled

The operator reviews the video, caption, privacy, and destination before explicitly posting.

About this application

Clip Pilot is an independently developed personal creator tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok, Google, YouTube, Meta, or Instagram.
